Conditions for participation in the Giveaway

All the conditions will remain unchanged until the prize draw takes place. The only change that we may make is increasing the number of prizes, which will depend on the number of the giveaway participants.

We pay for the shipping of the prize to the winner. The winner is responsible for any taxes that may result from winning. AEthing only pays for the prize and its delivery to the winner.

When each user you invite joins the giveaway, you receive one unique number, which will participate in the prize drawing.

The moment a new user you invited also joins the giveaway, a new record is made in the blockchain chain, which corresponds to a unique number.
This number is sent to your email. We recommend you to save all the numbers you receive. In case of loss you can enter your personal account on the website and get all the numbers you received.

Only unique users are counted (a unique user is one who has a unique email and phone number). Registration of various users with the same phone number and email address is not allowed.

We do not limit the quantity of the giveaway participation numbers that you can get, it all depends on how many new giveaway participants you invite. The more numbers you get, the higher are your
chances of winning.

We will inform you about the release date of our mobile application. The prize draw will take place on the same day.
