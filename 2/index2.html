Why is our giveaway based on blockchain technology?

In short, for two reasons: to ensure the fairness of the draw and to give people the opportunity to really influence the odds of winning!

The Internet is cluttered with various offers of winning different prizes

These doubts arise in each of us when we see calls for participation in various prize drawings.

Is the winner really chosen at random or is the winner known in advance?
Will the organizers of the giveaway change their minds and cancel it?
Are my chances to win exactly the same as other people?
Are there really fair prize draws?
I will not be able to influence my odds of winning?
Will the prize really be awarded to the winner?

In order to put all suspicions to rest and give anyone the opportunity to increase their chances of winning, we decided to implement this giveaway on blockchain technology!

Blockchain is a continuous chain of blocks. It contains all the records about who you invited to participate in our giveaway.

First, unlike in common databases, you cannot change or delete these records, you can only add new ones. This ensures that the data remains unchanged and that the results cannot be altered.

And secondly, in our giveaway you get the opportunity to influence your odds of winning!

Your chances to win grow in proportion to the number of friends you invite to participate in our drawing event.

Each new participant of the giveaway attracted by you generates a new record in the blockchain.

The more new users you invite, the more numbers participating in the drawing will you receive.
